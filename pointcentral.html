PointCentral

Year: 2026 Ⅰ Role: Lead Web & Brand Designer Ⅰ Focus: UX Strategy, Information Architecture, Visual Design System

Redesigning an enterprise property automation platform to improve navigation, brand consistency, and help users quickly identify the solutions most relevant to their business.

Challenge

As PointCentral expanded its solutions, the website needed a clearer structure and a more polished enterprise identity. Users needed a faster way to understand where they were and which solutions applied to them.

Solution

I redesigned the experience around a color-coded navigation system, unified page templates, and a refined visual language. These changes improved clarity while creating consistency across the entire digital ecosystem.

Impact

The result was a more intuitive and scalable experience that strengthened the brand, improved product discoverability, and better supported multiple audiences across the platform.
