Building36

Date: 2024

Role: Web Design

What it is

Building36 is a smart home platform that gives homeowners a simple way to control their home, optimize energy use, and stay connected with professional installers to ensure peak system performance.

The Challenge

The previous website lacked a clear product definition and user-focused messaging, making it difficult for users to quickly understand its value. I worked closely with the marketing team to clarify the target audience and core use cases, using those insights to guide a more intuitive and focused rebrand.
