Emails

My job also involves building emails for our partners, dealers, and other B2B clients using Salesforce Marketing Cloud, Pardot, and Constant Contact.

Beginning with stakeholder collaboration to grasp campaign objectives and target audiences, I design email mockups in Figma, refining them based on feedback. A/B testing aids in optimizing performance, directing enhancements for optimal impact. Once everyone's happy with the designs, I get down to coding them from scratch. I make sure they work well no matter what email app or web browser someone's using while viewing these email, in simple terms, I use my skills in design and coding to make emails that look great and get the job done for Alarm.com and our partners.













UI/UX

When it comes to websites or emails, the images and designs we use are crucial in showing off what the Alarm.com app can really do. That's where I come in, Collaborating closely with the UI/UX team, I receive Figma files to adjust them appropriately.

Imagine seeing a picture of a woman looking worried - you'd probably wonder what's got her so worked up, right? Well, that's why it's important to make sure our designs match the scene they're in.

By making sure the visuals fit perfectly with the content, we spark interest and keep people engaged. Each adjustment we make helps tell the story of the Alarm.com app in a way that's clear and compelling. It's all about working together to create designs that grab attention and make sense to folks using the app.
