Website

I've been redoing LDNS' whole website to match ASU branding. Using Drupal as their CMS, I've enjoyed diving into their brand guidelines to make the page more appealing and useful.

The original homepage was a bit confusing and didn't really show students what LDNS has to offer. After trying out different designs, they ended up picking the one that's live now as their main page. It's still a work in progress, but it's already making a big difference for students trying to find their way around the site. Keep an eye out for more updates as we keep tweaking those interior pages!
