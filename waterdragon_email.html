Get started with Early Access to ‍Alarm‍.com‍’s first "no plumber needed" solution for whole-home water protection
Preventing water damage has never been easier or more affordable than with the new award-winning smart water monitor, Water Dragon™!

The Water Dragon™ uses ultrasonic technology to monitor the water flow for an entire property, sending alerts for unexpected water flow. It can be securely attached to the main water line in minutes, making it ideal for residential and light commercial properties.
Key Features

Strength of ultrasound: The Water Dragon™ uses ultrasonic pulses to detect the flow of water and is able to monitor the water flow for an entire property.

Easy to install:  This device easily installs in under 10 minutes—no plumber required! Its compact design means it can be added even in tight spaces.*

Alerts and informs:  Customers can now easily detect unusual water flow, including hard-to-spot, slow leaks. The Water Dragon™ can even alert property owners to potential frozen pipes, preventing catastrophic water damage from burst pipes. Customers can view water trends for the day, week, or month through the mobile app to track usage over time and change their consumption to conserve water and save money.
This small but mighty device catches leaks before they become major issues, helping your customers avoid costly repairs. Pair the Water Dragon™ with a retrofit water valve, like the IQ Water Valve, for a complete “no plumber required” solution.

*A power source is needed to run the device.
